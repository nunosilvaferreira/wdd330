WDD 330 - Final Project Proposal: "Couple Time" App
==================================================

1. Overview
-----------
A therapeutic web app designed to help couples strengthen relationships through guided activities, now featuring Family Clinic's official brand colors (#005303 green, #FF9800 orange, #795548 brown) for visual consistency with their practice.

2. Target Audience
-----------------
Primary: Couples in therapy at Family Clinic (aligned with their green/orange/brown branding)
Secondary: Counselors tracking patient progress

3. Major Functions (8+)
-----------------------
1. Nature-Themed Activities ("Green" category)
2. Warm Conversation Starters ("Orange" category)  
3. Grounding Exercises ("Brown" category)
4. Mood Tracker with color-coded entries
5. Shared Gratitude Journal
6. Therapist Resources Portal
7. Offline Mode (localStorage)
8. Accessibility Mode (high contrast)
